Don't Worry available now!



My debut album, "Don't Worry", has been released. You can now stream or buy it on Bandcamp, or stream it on SoundCloud. I've been working on this album for about 5 months. Many hours were spent simply thinking of where to take the music and what kind of writing styles should be included. "Don't Worry" comes from a very personal place within me. Listen to the subtle guitar plucks and explosive verse transitions to get an idea of the contrast between what someone thinks versus what they do. I suppose this could be categorized as a concept album, but I'll leave that up to you.
